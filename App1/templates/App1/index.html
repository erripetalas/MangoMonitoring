Welcome to the Mango Monitoring Website

This website provides information about common pests and diseases affecting mango trees and fruits.

Why Monitor Mango Pests and Diseases?

Mangoes are one of the most important tropical fruits in the NT. Each year, mango production faces numerous challenges from various pests and diseases that can reduce yield and fruit quality.

Early detection and proper management of these pests and diseases can:

  • Increase crop yield and quality
  • Reduce economic losses
  • Minimize the use of pesticides
  • Promote sustainable farming practices
